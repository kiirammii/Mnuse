RULES

Welcome to the Escape Room. Here are some rules to help you get started!

  1. Every challenge has a help button with hints that can help you finish it.
  2. Time limit: There's a time limit for each room. If you run out of time, you'll have to restart that challenge.
  3. After you finish a challenge you have to click the "Save" button to save your progress, otherwise your progress won't be saved!
  4. Pay attention to the details: Even the smallest clue can be crucial to solving the challenges! There's also easter eggs hidden everywhere.
